# Mount 2000 Youth Retreat 2027 — Organization Guide

**Organization:** Mount Saint Mary Seminary (Mount 2000 / MSMS)
**Event:** Mount 2000 Youth Retreat 2027
**Dates:** July 9–12, 2027
**Location:** Mount St. Mary's University, 16300 Old Emmitsburg Road, Emmitsburg, MD 21727
**Capacity:** 1,000 participants
**Registration Type:** Group Registration Only (parishes register as a group)

---

## How Registration Works

Registration for this event is done by **group leaders** (typically a youth minister or parish contact). Each parish submits one registration form on behalf of their entire group. Individual participant details are collected separately through **liability forms** after the group registers.

### Step 1 — Group Leader Registers the Group

The group leader visits the registration page and fills out:

**Section 1: Parish & Group Leader Information**

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Group Name | Yes | e.g., "St. Mary's Youth Group" |
| Parish Name | Yes | e.g., "St. Mary's Catholic Church" |
| Diocese Name | Yes | e.g., "Diocese of Baltimore" |
| Group Leader Full Name | Yes | |
| Group Leader Email | Yes | All communications go here |
| Group Leader Phone | Yes | |
| Group Leader Street Address | Yes | |
| Group Leader City | Yes | |
| Group Leader State | Yes | Dropdown — all 50 states |
| Group Leader ZIP Code | Yes | 5-digit ZIP |

**Section 2: Alternative Contacts**

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Primary Alternative Contact Name | Yes | Backup contact if leader unreachable |
| Primary Alternative Contact Email | Yes | |
| Primary Alternative Contact Phone | Yes | |
| Secondary Alternative Contact Name | No | Optional third contact |
| Secondary Alternative Contact Email | No | |
| Secondary Alternative Contact Phone | No | |

**Section 3: Participant Counts**

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Youth Count | Yes | Number of youth attending |
| Chaperone Count | Yes | Number of adult chaperones |
| Priest Count | No | Number of priests (free of charge) |

> Note: Specific participant names, ages, and medical info are collected later via liability forms — not at this stage.

**Section 4: Ticket Type & Housing**

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| General Admission | Full event access (Thu–Sun) with housing selection |
| Day Pass | Single-day attendance only (if enabled) |

For General Admission, the group also selects their **housing type**:

| Housing Type | Youth Price | Chaperone Price |
|-------------|-------------|-----------------|
| On-Campus Housing | $105.00 | $80.00 |
| Off-Campus (Self-Arranged) | $80.00 | $65.00 |

**Section 5: Additional Information**

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Special Requests | No | Open text for group needs |
| Coupon Code | No | Only shown if organizer enables coupons |

**Section 6: Terms Agreement**

The group leader must check a box agreeing to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, and acknowledging that liability forms must be completed for all participants.

---

### Step 2 — Review & Payment

After filling out the form, the group leader is taken to a **Review page** where they can confirm all details before paying.

**Payment is processed securely via Stripe.**

For this event, a **$25.00 per-person deposit** is due at registration. The remaining balance is due before the event.

---

### Step 3 — Liability Forms (Per Participant)

After registration, the group leader receives a link to submit a **liability form for every individual participant** (youth and chaperones). These forms collect:

**Medical Information**
- Medical conditions
- Current medications
- Allergies
- Dietary restrictions
- ADA / accessibility accommodations

**Emergency Contacts**
- Emergency Contact 1: Name, Phone, Relationship
- Emergency Contact 2: Name, Phone, Relationship (optional)

**Insurance Information**
- Insurance Provider
- Policy Number
- Group Number

**Consent / Waiver**
- General liability waiver acknowledgment

> Liability forms are required for all participants before they can be checked in at the event.

## What Happens After Registration

Once a group registers, the following tools are available to the **Mount 2000 team** in the admin dashboard:

### POROS Portal (Planning & Operations)

The POROS portal is the event management hub. It handles:

| Feature | What It Does |
|---------|-------------|
| Housing Assignments | Assigns youth groups to dormitory rooms by gender |
| Meal Color Groups | Assigns groups a color (Red, Blue, Green, etc.) that determines their meal time |
| Small Groups (SGLs) | Assigns each group a Seminarian Small Group Leader |
| Seating Arrangements | Organizes seating for talks and Mass |
| ADA Accommodations | Tracks participants needing accessibility support |
| Priest Housing | Separate housing management for priests |
| Religious Staff | Tracks seminarians and religious attending |

### Salve Check-In

At arrival, participants are checked in using the **Salve check-in system** — a QR-code or name-based lookup that confirms the participant has a completed liability form and marks them as arrived.

### Rapha Medical

The **Rapha medical module** gives the on-site medical team access to participant medical records, allergies, and emergency contacts for the duration of the event.

### Public POROS Portal

A **participant-facing portal** is enabled for this event. Group leaders and participants can log in to see:
- Their room assignments
- Meal color and meal times
- Small group schedules
- Event schedule (Friday–Sunday)
- Campus maps and resources

## Optional / Admin-Enabled Features

These features can be turned on or off by the Mount 2000 admin team:

| Feature | Default State |
|---------|--------------|
| Coupon Codes | Off (can be enabled) |
| Day Pass Ticket Type | Off (can be enabled for single-day groups) |
| On-Campus Capacity Limit | Not set (unlimited unless configured) |
| Off-Campus Capacity Limit | Not set (unlimited unless configured) |
| Custom Registration Questions | None (admin can add from the question catalog) |

### Available Custom Question Catalog

The admin can add any of the following optional questions to the registration form at any time:

**Parish & Diocese Info**
- What diocese are you from?
- What parish do you belong to?
- Who is your pastor?
- What state are you coming from?

**Demographics (per individual)**
- What language(s) do you speak? *(English, Spanish, Both, Other)*
- What grade are you in? *(6th–12th, College, N/A)*
- T-shirt size
- Gender *(Male/Female)*
- Age

**Event Experience**
- Has your group attended this event before? *(Yes/No)*
- Approximately how many first-time attendees are in your group?

**Faith & Sacraments**
- What sacraments have you received?
- Will you need a gluten-free communion host?
- Are you currently involved in youth ministry?
- Are you interested in volunteering during the event?

**Logistics**
- How will you be getting to the event? *(Driving, Bus/charter, Carpool, Flying, Other)*
- Estimated arrival date and time
- Estimated departure date and time

**Dietary & Accessibility**
- Dietary restrictions *(multi-select: Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-free, Dairy-free, Nut allergy, None)*
- Additional food allergies (open text)
- Accessibility needs (open text)

**Additional Notes**
- Is there anything else you would like us to know?
- Prayer intentions
